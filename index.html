How to use

Three ways to set up a search — pick whichever's easiest.

All three end up in exactly the same place: a saved search that alerts you on Telegram.

Method 01

Paste a link

Copy any OLX search or category link straight from your browser. OlxMonitor reads the city, keyword, and price filter off it automatically.

https://www.olx.in/lucknow_g4059306/q-mac-mini-m4?filter=price_between_30000_to_60000
Method 02

One-line command

Know exactly what you want? Type it all in a single message and you're subscribed immediately.

/subscribe Lucknow | mac mini m4 | 40000-65000
Method 03

Guided chat

Not sure of the syntax? Send /subscribe alone and OlxMonitor asks you one question at a time.

OlxMonitor: Which city should I search in?
You: Lucknow
OlxMonitor: What should I search for?
You: mac mini m4

FAQ

Questions people actually ask

How fast is "instant," really?

OlxMonitor checks OLX twice a day on a fixed schedule, not continuously. It's fast enough to beat someone who checks manually once a day, but it isn't a live, second-by-second feed. If a listing appears and sells within minutes of going live, OlxMonitor may not catch it in time.

Do I need a card to start?

No. The 7-day trial needs nothing but a Telegram account. When the trial ends, you pay by UPI directly — no card is ever stored or requested.

Can I track more than one search?

Yes — add as many searches as you like (different cities, keywords, or budgets), all under one account and one trial/subscription. There's no per-search charge.

What happens when my plan expires?

Your searches stay saved but go quiet. OlxMonitor sends one payment reminder a day until you renew — it won't spam you, and nothing is deleted while you decide.

Is this affiliated with OLX?

No. OlxMonitor is an independent tool that reads publicly visible OLX listings on your behalf. It isn't operated, endorsed, or supported by OLX.

Is my payment automated?

No — deliberately. You pay by UPI and report the transaction id; a real person confirms it and reactivates your account by hand. It's slower than a card, but it means no auto-billing and no stored payment details.
