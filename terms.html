1. Acceptance of terms

By using the OlxMonitor Telegram bot ("OlxMonitor", "we", "the service"), you agree to these terms. If you don't agree, please don't use the service.

2. What OlxMonitor is — and isn't

OlxMonitor is an independent, unofficial tool that periodically checks publicly visible OLX search results against filters you set, and notifies you on Telegram when something matches. OlxMonitor is:

  • Not affiliated with OLX India in any way — we don't operate, endorse, or have any special access to OLX's platform beyond what any visitor can see.
  • Not affiliated with Telegram FZ-LLC — we use Telegram's public Bot API like any other developer.
  • Not a marketplace, escrow service, or party to any transaction — any deal you make with a seller you find through OlxMonitor is strictly between you and them.

3. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old and have a valid Telegram account to use OlxMonitor, since the service involves real payments.

4. Free trial and subscription

  • New accounts get a 7-day free trial, starting from your first search.
  • The trial covers every search on your account — there's no per-search charge.
  • After the trial, continued access costs ₹99 per 30 days (subject to change with notice — see Section 9).
  • Payment is manual: you pay via UPI and report the transaction id with /verify; a human confirms and reactivates your account. There is no automatic card billing and no auto-renewal — if you don't pay, your searches simply stay paused.
  • If your trial or plan lapses, your searches remain saved but stop sending alerts. You'll get a reminder once a day until you either pay or remove your searches.

5. Refunds

Payments are manually verified and applied to your account for the period stated at checkout. Because verification is manual and typically completed within hours, refund requests are handled case-by-case — contact us if something's gone wrong.

OlxMonitor relies entirely on OLX's publicly available listing data, which we don't control. As a result:

  • We don't guarantee that any listing is accurate, available, genuine, or free of scams.
  • We don't guarantee OlxMonitor will catch every matching listing, or catch one before it's sold — checks run on a schedule, not continuously.
  • OLX may change its site in ways that temporarily or permanently break OlxMonitor's ability to find listings.
  • You are solely responsible for verifying any listing and any seller before completing a transaction.
